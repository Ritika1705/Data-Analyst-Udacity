KEY INSIGHTS:¶

The loan dataset has a large number columns from which the following conclusions can be drawn:

We can clearly see that BorrowerAPR is a better measure than BorrowerRate to calculate LenderYield and other important factor.

Most of the people issuing loans are from Grade C and Grade D category and their income range is between 25000$ - 49999$

Greater the prosper score lesser is the risk of being at loss.
